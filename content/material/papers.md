### Nonholonomic Constraints
These papers by M. Flannery discuss the issues that arise if we try to couple nonholonomic constraints to the
Lagrangian. Flannery shows that it is not possible to define a variational principle in such cases, but d'Alembert's
principle (or generalizations of it) still holds.

**M. Flannery**, [*The enigma of nonholonomic constraints*](<../pdfs/Flannery2005_The enigma of nonholonomic constraints.pdf>), American Journal of Physics **73**, 265 (2005)

**M. Flannery**, [*The elusive d'Alembert-Lagrange dynamics of nonholonomic systems*](<../pdfs/Flannery2011_The elusive d’Alembert-Lagrange dynamics of nonholonomic systems.pdf>), American Journal of Physics **79**, 932 (2011)

**M. Flannery**, [*D’Alembert–Lagrange analytical dynamics for nonholonomic systems*](<../pdfs/Flannery2011_d’Alembert–Lagrange analytical dynamics for nonholonomic systems.pdf>), Journal of Mathematical Physics **52**, 032705 (2011)
